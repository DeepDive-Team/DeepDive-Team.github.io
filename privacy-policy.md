# Privacy Policy


## Collected Data

In order to provide its core functionality, **DeepDive collects responses generated by ChatGPT while the extension is enabled**. This data is subject to the privacy policies of [Vercel](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy) and [Google Gemini](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13594961?hl=en#), which are used to fulfill the services of the extension.

DeepDive does not store any of the collected data, nor does it store any of the data that it provides to users.

## Logged Metrics

DeepDive *does* track when:
- A survey is sent ("Was this response helpful?")
- A search suggestion is liked or disliked
- Search suggestions are generated
- An analysis is done on a suggested search

The suggestions and analyses themselves are **not stored**.
