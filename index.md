# Why DeepDive?

AI generated responses are prone to errors, but it is annoying and tedious to check them. 

In order to solve this, DeepDive does the work for you, providing easy Google search suggestions which are a perfect starting point for you to do your own research. 

Along with these suggestions, DeepDive will also analyze the results of those searches and help you understand which ones reliable.

# How do I use it?

It's easy! Simply have a conversation with ChatGPT while the extension is enabled, and DeepDive does the rest. If a response is deemed to contain important factual information, two search suggestions will be provided.

One of these suggestions pertains directly to the topic. The second suggestion takes on a broader perspective in order to help you see the bigger picture or the core understanding.
